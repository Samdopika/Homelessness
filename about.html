What our Team Does

The Houselessness Service Org. is a mutual aid group providing food, clothing, sanitary products, and resources to unhoused folks in San Diego and educating the public about issues surrounding the housing crisis. Our group student run and completely donation based!

More About Us

Our organization, UCSD Houslessness Service Organization, strives to provide our houseless community, primarily in the downtown area, with essentials such as food, clothing, hygiene products, blankets, and water. We also provide resource pamphlets that contain information such as legal and housing resources. More than anything, as student activists and advocates we aim to build connections with our unhoused folk and get to know them as they get to know us as well. In terms of the logistics, our organization acquires funding by garnering donations, from booths and events that we hold on campus, and with that we buy the food and hygiene supplies, etc. that we need. Things like clothing, shoes, and blankets are usually collected through donations, and then we organize these by item type. People often just take what they need and will ask us if they are in need of something specific. Most of the time, all the warmer items are being taken right away, and in general we don’t see enough larger sizes for clothing. Lately with food we have started preparing hot meals while also picking up food items from grocery stores that they don’t plan on keeping. This is a little insight on how we do the work that we do and will continue to do for our houseless community.
