about Houselessness

The Houselessness Service Organization (HSO) is a student-run, donation-based group in San Diego, committed to assisting the unhoused community by providing essentials like food, clothing, and sanitary products. It also connects individuals to critical resources inlcuding medical services and employment opportunities. Besides direct aid, HSO is also dedicated to raising awareness about the housing crisis, educating the public about its causes and implications, and advocating for systemic change to address the issue.

Mission causes

We are committed to helping the homeless in San Diego with a variety of resources.

Promoting Community Involvement

Our team fosters a culture of compassion within the community, inspiring individuals, businesses, and organizations to participate in addressing the housing crisis.

Alleviating Immediate Needs

Our team aims to provide essential items, such as food, clothing, and sanitary products, to improve the quality of life for the unhoused population in San Diego.

Providing Resources

Our team is dedicated to linking unhoused individuals with vital resources like medical services, legal aid, and employment opportunities to empower them towards stability.

Public Awareness

Our team works tirelessly to educate the public about the housing crisis, driving awareness of its root causes, societal impacts, and potential prevention strategies.

Advocating for Systemic Change

Our Team advocates for policy reform and preventive strategies, aiming to influence decision-makers to prioritize and address housing issues sustainably.

Empowering Student Volunteers
