How Donations Help

Donations lay the foundation of the UCSD Homelessness Service Organization, empowering us to make a tangible difference in the lives of our houseless community in downtown San Diego. With your generous contributions, we can provide essential resources such as food, clothing, hygiene products, blankets, and water. Your monetary donations enable us to purchase these items in bulk, ensuring a steady supply for those in need. Additionally, donations of food, hygiene supplies, clothing, shoes, and blankets help us meet the specific needs of individuals experiencing homelessness. By donating, you help us foster connections, advocate for change, and provide vital support to those who need it most. Together, we can create a more compassionate and inclusive society, one donation at a time.
